Vanessa Thompson

Vanessa is a UC Berkeley student studying applied statistics and economics. Her goal for the future is to use her mathematics and analytics background to work for environmental and social causes. Her drive to mitigate climate change came from her travels to 42 nations before the age of 18. As a world traveler, artist, and mathematician, Vanessa writes about new methods for environmental, educational, minority, and youth activism on the Huffington Post. She hopes to explore the world of coding and data analysis for social good throughout college and her career.
